Half Cooked Maggi

Description

This is a really cool way of cooking maggi.I call it the half cooked maggi.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • One 13 rupee packet of maggi

Steps

  1. Take as less water as you can in a pan.
  2. Turn on the stove.
  3. Open the packet of maggi and get the masala out.
  4. Spread a little bit of masala in the hot water.
  5. Breaking into two pieces put maggi in the pan.
  6. Spread the remaining masala on it.
  7. Wait 2 minutes and 2 minutes only this gives it the uncooked USP.
  8. Serve
